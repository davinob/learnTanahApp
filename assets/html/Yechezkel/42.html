And before the chambers was a walk of ten cubits breadth inward, a way of one cubit; and their doors were towards the north.

Now the upper chambers were shorter: for the galleries took away from these, more than from the lower, and the middlemost, in the building.

When the priests enter there, then shall they not go out of the holy place into the outer court, but there they shall lay their garments in which they minister; for they are holy; and shall put on other garments, and shall approach to those things which belong to the people.
