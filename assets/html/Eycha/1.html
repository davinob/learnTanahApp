'For these things I weep; Mine eye, mine eye runneth down with water; Because the comforter is far from me, Even he that should refresh my soul; My children are desolate, Because the enemy hath prevailed.' פ⁠

J’ai appelé ceux qui m’aimaient: ce sont eux qui m’ont leurrée! Mes prêtres et mes vieillards ont expiré dans la ville, car vainement ils demandaient de la nourriture pour ranimer leur vie.
I called for my lovers, But they deceived me; My priests and mine elders Perished in the city, While they sought them food To refresh their souls. ר⁠
